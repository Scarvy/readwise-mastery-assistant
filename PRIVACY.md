# Privacy Policy

Readwise Mastery Assistant is an unofficial, personal browser extension. It
has no backend server, and does not collect, store, or transmit any data to
its developer.

## What data the extension accesses

When you click "✨ Suggest Q&A cards" on a Readwise review page, the
extension reads:

- The highlight's text
- Your note on that highlight (if any)
- The source title and author

## How that data is used

This content is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you've
configured (Anthropic or OpenAI), using your own API key, solely to generate
Q&A flashcard suggestions. See
[Anthropic's privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy) or
[OpenAI's privacy policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy) for how
they handle that data.

## What is stored, and where

Your API key(s), selected provider, and model preferences are stored locally
in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`. This data never leaves your
device except as part of the API request described above, and is never sent
to any server operated by this extension's developer.

## Data sharing

This extension does not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third
party other than the AI provider you explicitly configure, and only for the
purpose of generating flashcard suggestions.
